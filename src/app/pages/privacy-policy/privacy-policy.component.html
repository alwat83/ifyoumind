1. Introduction

IfYouMind (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a community platform for discovering, sharing, and refining ideas that make the world better. We operate under Dose Ninja Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in compliance with applicable laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA).

By using IfYouMind, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

(a) Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and optional profile fields (photo, bio, display name).
  • Content Data: Ideas, comments, upvotes, bookmarks, and any materials you submit.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send us (feedback, support tickets, reports).

(b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage & Technical Data: IP address, device and browser information, pages viewed, actions taken, referral URLs.
  • Log Files: Diagnostic logs and timestamps for security and reliability.
  • Analytics: Aggregated engagement metrics (e.g., popular pages, feature usage).

(c) Information from Third-Party Services

  • Single Sign-On (SSO): If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email, and profile image (no passwords).
  • Payments (if applicable): When you purchase a subscription, Stripe processes payment data as a separate controller/processor. We receive limited billing metadata (e.g., status, plan) — not full card details.

3. How We Use Information

  1. Operate, maintain, and improve the platform and its community features.
  2. Personalize your experience and suggest relevant content.
  3. Provide support, communicate updates, and send administrative messages.
  4. Moderate content, enforce our Terms of Conduct, and prevent misuse or fraud.
  5. Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

We do not sell personal information. We may use de-identified or aggregated data for analytics and product improvement.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contractual necessity: To deliver features you request (accounts, posts, comments).
  • Legitimate interests: To secure, debug, and improve the service; prevent abuse; understand usage.
  • Consent: For optional cookies, certain communications, or specific data uses.
  • Legal obligation: To meet regulatory, tax, or law-enforcement requirements.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (hosting, authentication, database, analytics) and other vendors under data-processing agreements; Stripe for payments (if used).
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of users and the public.
  • Corporate Events: In a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, data may transfer subject to this Policy.

Vendors are contractually obligated to handle data securely and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as your account is active or as needed for legitimate business purposes (security, legal, archival). When feasible, we anonymize user identifiers on public content after account deletion to preserve conversation context without exposing identity.

7. International Transfers

Data is processed in the United States via Google Cloud/Firebase. If you are located outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, and processed in, a jurisdiction with different data-protection laws. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

8. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Role-based access and Firebase Security Rules.
  • Least-privilege access by authorized personnel under confidentiality obligations.

No online service can guarantee 100% security. You acknowledge and accept this risk when using the platform.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Submit requests to {{ contactEmail }}. We strive to respond within 30 days.

10. Children’s Privacy

IfYouMind is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and local storage to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and measure performance. You can manage cookies via your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

12. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Last Updated” date will be revised. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
