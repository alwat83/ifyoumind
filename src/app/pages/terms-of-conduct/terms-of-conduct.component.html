🤝 Terms of Conduct

Our mission is to cultivate a respectful, open, and inspiring environment for sharing and improving ideas that positively impact the world. These guidelines help us create that space.

💬 Respect & Civility

Treat every member with kindness, empathy, and respect. We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or hate speech. Assume good faith and engage in discussions with positive intent.

💡 Constructive Contribution

Provide thoughtful, actionable feedback. Focus on the substance of the idea, not the person presenting it. Avoid spam, off-topic remarks, and excessive self-promotion.

©️ Intellectual Property

Share only content you own or have explicit permission to use. By posting, you grant IfYouMind a non-exclusive license to display and manage your content on the platform.

🔒 Privacy & Transparency

Protect the privacy of others. Do not share personal or identifying information without consent. Impersonation is not permitted. Use your real identity or a consistent pseudonym.

⚖️ Ethical Use

Do not use our platform for illegal activities, spreading misinformation, or manipulation. Clearly label AI-generated content. Report violations to community@ifyoumind.com.

🛡️ Moderation & Enforcement

IfYouMind reserves the right to remove content or suspend accounts that violate these terms. Severe or repeated violations may lead to a permanent ban from the community.

These guidelines are a living document. Suggest improvements by contacting feedback@ifyoumind.com.
